Individual training program

Individualized Training Programs (ITPs) are concentrated, practical, one-to-one coaching sessions conducted for individuals who wish to receive the full-time attention of specialized and experienced subject matter experts.

These ITPs are specifically designed to help individuals bridge specific competency gaps or acquire new skills in short periods of time, usually ranging between 1 and 5 days, depending on individual and organizational needs. The subjects covered in such ITPs are based on the specific needs of each participant and comprise briefings by pure visions subject matter experts, reviews of manuals, reports and other data, as well as supervised exercises and structured discussions. These ITPs can be conducted in English and/or Arabic.

For more information about our experience in ITPs, write to us citing ITP in your email and a Partner-In-Charge will be in touch with you shortly after to explore your requirement and provide you with the answers and/or the proposal you require. We will then work with you to identify the ideal ITP methodology to help you achieve your desired development goals.
